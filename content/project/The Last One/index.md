## Overview

**The Last One** is a first-person shooter that blends historical exploration with sci-fi elements, set in the mysterious ruins near the ancient Indus Valley civilization.

## Story

You love to explore the historical treasures of your country. While exploring ancient civilizations near the Indus Valley, you start noticing strange activities.

You hear about a legend that says 10,000 years ago, aliens left unique crystals in these ruins. Armed with determination and your weapons, you begin your search for these legendary crystals.

## Gameplay Features

- **First-Person Combat**: Engage enemies with multiple weapon types
- **Exploration**: Navigate through historically-inspired environments
- **Crystal Hunt**: Find and collect mysterious alien artifacts
- **Atmospheric Levels**: Explore detailed ancient ruins with sci-fi elements

## Technical Details

- **Engine**: Unity
- **Genre**: First-Person Shooter / Adventure
- **Platform**: Windows (PC) + Browser (WebGL)
- **Controls**:
    - Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
    - Weapon Switch: 1-4 Keys
    - Shoot: Left Click
    - Aim: Right Click
    - Sprint: Shift + W

## Development Highlights

- Implemented FPS controller with smooth movement and aiming mechanics
- Created multiple weapon systems with different behaviors
- Designed historically-inspired environment with sci-fi elements
- Developed WebGL version for browser play (with optimizations)
- Added cinematic cutscenes in standalone version

## Platform Differences

- **PC Standalone**: Full experience with cutscenes and enhanced graphics
- **Browser Version**: Optimized WebGL build with streamlined performance
