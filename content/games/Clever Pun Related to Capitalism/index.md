## Overview

**Clever Pun Related to Capitalism** is a satirical puzzle game created in just 48 hours for GMTK Game Jam 2024. Players must balance an ever-growing skyscraper while following increasingly absurd corporate demands.

## Gameplay

Corporate has demanded you play this game! Slide floors and balance your skyscraper as corporate gives you more and more unreasonable orders. The game satirizes corporate culture through engaging puzzle mechanics and witty design.
